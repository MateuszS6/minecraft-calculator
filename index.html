Nether Portals

Convert dimension coordinates to find the best placement to link portals.
Things to note:

  • Coordinates in the Nether are 1/8th of the Overworld coordinates.
  • Y coordinate is the same in both dimensions (clamped to world limits).
  • Portals can link in both directions, so you can use this tool to convert Nether to Overworld as well.

Simply enter the coordinates in the Overworld or Nether, and the tool will calculate the corresponding coordinates in the other dimension!
